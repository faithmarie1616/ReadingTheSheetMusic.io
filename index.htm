Reading the Sheet Music

When listening to a song most people don't tend to imagine the process of how the song came to be. The same can be said for websites, meaning that many people don't go on websites and consider the coding that created the website. Unless you've studied music or coding, you'll most likely just enjoy what you're listening to or looking at. When looking at the sheet music of a song, you're allowing yourself to see the bits and pieces of each note. This can also expose hidden vocals or harmonies that you may not have noticed before.

When making your own webpage, it's important to use references from other sites to avoid blindly working on one yourself. Even with years of experience, looking at another source code could potentially cut down on the amount of time it takes to create your own. Although I didn't look at source code when making my websites, I would've benefited from doing so. It's important to see how things that surround our daily lives are made because it really allows us to get a better understanding of the world.
